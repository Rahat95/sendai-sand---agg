Our Products

We supply premium quality construction materials tested for strength, durability, and consistency to meet all your project requirements.

River Sand

Premium quality natural river sand with optimal grain size distribution.

Usage: Ideal for plastering and masonry work
Grade: Fine to medium grain

M-Sand (Manufactured Sand)

High-quality manufactured sand with consistent particle size and shape.

Usage: Best for RCC and concrete applications
Grade: Uniform and angular particles

Aggregates (10mm / 20mm / 40mm)

High-strength construction aggregates available in multiple sizes.

Usage: Concrete mixing and structural applications
Sizes: 10mm, 20mm, 40mm available

Stone Chips

Clean and durable crushed stone chips for various construction needs.

Usage: Road construction and concrete mixing
Quality: Clean, angular, and durable

GSB & Filling Material

Graded stone base and filling materials for infrastructure projects.

Usage: Road and infrastructure projects
Application: Base course and filling work
